Dust:

  • Coal dust - used as fuel in furnace
  • Iron dust - used to sharpen swords
  • Diamond dust - used to sharpen swords, like iron dust but better

Melee weapons:

  • Wooden bat - deals 3 damage, has 110 durability
  • Stone mace - deals 6 damage, has 75 durability
  • Spear - deals 7 damage, has 30 durability
  • Iron sword - deals 8 damage, has 250 durability, can be upgraded

Shards:

  • Diamond shard - used to upgrade iron armor
  • Emerald shard - used to upgrade iron armor, better than diamond shard
  • Ruby shard - used to upgrade iron armor, better than emerald shard

Upgrade slots:

  • Upgrade slot (empty) - used to craft other upgrade slots
  • Upgrade slot (diamond) - used to upgrade iron armor
  • Upgrade slot (emerald) - used to upgrade iron armor, better than diamond
  • Upgrade slot (ruby) - used to upgrade iron armor, better than emerald
